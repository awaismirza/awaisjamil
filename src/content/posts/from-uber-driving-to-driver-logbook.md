title: From Uber Driving to Building Driver Logbook

date: 2026-01-01
category: Indie Apps
---

Some product ideas come from market research. Others come from dealing with the same annoying problem every week.

Driver Logbook came from the second category.

As a rideshare driver, it is easy to track income at a high level, but harder to understand real profit after expenses, receipts, fuel, maintenance, and tax-time records.

I wanted a simple app that made income and expense tracking feel quick enough to actually use. No spreadsheet. No complicated accounting system. Just a practical mobile tool built around the way drivers already work.

The lesson is simple: useful products often start with a problem you personally understand.
