---
title: Designing for useful velocity
date: 2026-06-12
description: Speed matters most when the product team is pointed at the right constraint.
---

Useful velocity is not the number of tickets closed in a sprint. It is the rate at
which a team removes uncertainty from the product and gives customers something
better to use.

The practical move is to make the next build small enough to ship and specific
enough to teach you something. That usually means fewer features, stronger
workflow states, and a sharper definition of what success looks like.
